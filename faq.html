REGISTRATION


1. What are the qualifications for becoming a BMW White Card member?

BMW Owners who have bought their cars and/or motorcycles from BMW Authorised Dealers are entitled to be a BMW White Card member. BMW Owners who purchased their BMW car(s) from 1 April 2008 onwards and/or motorcycle(s) from 1 April 2012 onwards will have their BMW White Card sent to them automatically. If you are not yet a BMW White Card member and your car and/ or motorcycle was registered with JPJ within the last 3 years and it was initially purchased from a BMW Authorised Dealer, please call BMW Voice at 1 800 88 3000 for more info.

2. How would I know if my application for BMW White Card is successful?

You will be sent your BMW White Card and this will serve as proof of your membership. You may contact BMW Voice at 1 800 88 3000 to enquire about the status of your application 2 - 4 weeks after your date of submission.

3. What privileges and benefits can I look forward to?

As a BMW White Card member, you are entitled to exclusive privileges and offers. You can enjoy privileges for your car(s) and/or motorcycles, for yourself and your family members such as sneak previews on selected car launches and invites to BMW events such as BMW Golf Cup International, among others. Also, we are partnering with the best brands in town to give you more exclusive offers and events.

4. Do I need to pay to be a BMW White Card member?

For the first 3 years from the initial JPJ registration, it is free. To extend your membership and to continue enjoying all of the exclusive privileges, you only have to pay a minimal fee of RM300 each year thereafter, until the 6th year from your car’s registration date. If there are no new BMW purchases after the 6th year, your BMW White Card membership will be fully terminated.

5. Is the BMW White Card membership transferable?

This programme is specially customised for you. As such, the BMW White Card membership is non-transferable and is specifically for the registered owner or beneficiary of the BMW car.

ACCOUNT EXPIRY AND RENEWAL


1. When will my BMW White Card membership expire?

The expiry date on your BMW White Card will determine your membership's expiry.

2. How do I renew my BMW White Card membership?

Call BMW Voice at 1 800 88 3000 to request membership renewal. A fee of RM300, payable by credit card, is charged for yearly renewal. In the event that your membership is not renewed, please call BMW Voice and we will attend to the issue immediately.

3. If I sell my BMW, am I still a BMW White Card member?

You will still get to enjoy the benefits of being a member until the expiry date of your BMW White Card. That said, we are inclined to invite the new owner of your car into the programme. So it would be much appreciated if you could update your car's details at whitecard.bmw.com.my or call BMW Voice at 1 800 88 3000 to share your friend's/buyer's details with us. Also, remember to transfer the warranty card details to your friend/buyer as well, as he/she will need this when he/she purchases your car.

BMW WHITE CARD ONLINE PORTAL


1. What can I do online?

We have created an online account for you to enable you to manage your account at your own convenience. Upon login, you can:
• update certain personal details.
• view the latest offers and news from BMW Malaysia.

2. How do I log in?

In your BMW White Card welcome pack, you will be provided with a default password.

3. What do I do if I lose my password?

Call BMW Voice at 1 800 88 3000.

4. Can I change my password?

Certainly. Just log on to whitecard.bmw.com.my to do so.
